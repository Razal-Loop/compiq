Why one firm covers all three

The skill underneath is the same: reading a source text precisely and structuring evidence around it.

A regulator's audit checklist, an evaluator's scoring rubric, and a Part-145 inspection all reward the same thing — a document that maps cleanly onto what the reader is checking for. ComplDoc treats bids, compliance files, and manuals as the same discipline applied to three different reading audiences.

ComplDoc is

  • A documentation-first practice, not a law firm
  • Built from primary source text — regulation, RFP, or manual standard
  • Structured to match how the document gets evaluated

ComplDoc is not

  • A law firm, notified body, or regulator
  • A certification or accreditation authority
  • A generic proposal template mill
Who this is for
